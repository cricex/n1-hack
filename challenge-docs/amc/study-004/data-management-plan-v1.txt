Data management plan, v1

Data collection and sources
- Sources: EHR, registries
- Data types: de_identified

Storage and access
- Storage location: internal workspace, to be confirmed
- Access model: least privilege for study staff, to be confirmed

Retention
- Retention period: not specified in intake, requires clarification
- Disposal: secure deletion and access revocation at study end, to be confirmed

Logging
- Recommend minimal operational logs: timestamps, job success or failure, and non sensitive identifiers
- Avoid storing raw prompts or outputs containing PHI unless explicitly required and approved

Sharing
- Sharing indicated: internal
- If external sharing occurs, define transfer method, encryption, recipient restrictions, and agreement requirements
